How It Works

1. Faculty Creates Teams

Faculty members create project teams and add up to 4 students using their USN (University Seat Numbers).

2. Team Leader Assignment

Faculty designates one team member as the leader. Login credentials are automatically generated and emailed to the leader.

3. Project Submission

Team leaders login and submit project reports (PDF) along with GitHub repository links.

4. AI Evaluation

Projects are evaluated by Gemini AI based on faculty-defined criteria, providing detailed feedback and marks.

5. Faculty Review

Faculty members review AI evaluations, provide their own assessment, and finalize marks.

6. Results Publication

Students can view comprehensive results including AI marks, faculty marks, and detailed feedback.
